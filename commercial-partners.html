Why Partner with TellCo Europe?

TellCo Europe helps partners move beyond transactional product sales by giving them the tools to sell broader, higher‑value solutions across lighting, solar, efficiency, and distributed‑energy applications.

1

Growing Market

The global LED lighting market is projected to reach USD 130–133 billion by 2030, with indoor and outdoor solar LED segments expanding rapidly. This gives commercial partners a large and growing market for TellCo’s lighting and solar portfolios.

2

Real Solutions

TellCo enables partners to move beyond simple product reselling and offer complete solution based packages across LED indoor, outdoor, and public lighting; solar PV; off grid systems; and smart energy applications.

3

Revenue Potential

TellCo Europe’s commercial partner model is designed to help channel partners accelerate sales, strengthen customer credibility, and grow revenue across multiple solution categories.

Commercial Partner Ecosystem

Two paths for local market growth

TellCo Europe’s commercial partner model is organized around two main pathways: reseller-based partnerships and franchise partnerships. Resellers support market access, customer acquisition, and product distribution. Franchise partners operate with a broader local business model that may include showroom presence, CRM access, e-commerce support, and country or regional representation.

Resellers

Resellers, distributors, dealers, and agents

Reseller-based commercial partners help TellCo Europe reach customers through trusted local sales channels.

Benefits

Attractive commissions — Earnings of up to 23% of total sales achieved by the reseller partner, depending on program level.
Strategic support and training — Comprehensive product training, design‑simulation guidance, project‑planning support, and technology workshops to build strong technical capabilities.
Discounts, rebates and incentives — Resellers benefit from pricing discounts, tier‑based rebates, and other incentives designed to improve margins and support long‑term growth.
Sales and marketing support — Assistance with proposals, pre‑sales engineering, sales presentations, and creation of marketing materials to strengthen commercial performance.
Short, renewable agreement — Initial one year agreement, renewable after performance assessment, giving partners flexibility and a clear path for continued collaboration.
Brand reputation — Operate under TellCo Europe’s global brand, recognized for expertise and a broad range of LED and solar solutions.

Franchise Model

Franchise: Build a stronger local business presence

The SolarLED Franchise model is designed for qualified entrepreneurs and companies that want to build a stronger local business presence around TellCo Europe’s solar, LED, off-grid power, and energy-efficiency solutions.

Benefits

Structured franchise models for solar and LED — Dedicated franchise frameworks provide a proven operational blueprint for establishing and expanding local solar and LED operations.
Exclusive territories or segments — Franchise agreements define protected territories or market segments, ensuring clear market boundaries and strong local positioning.
Strategic planning and project support — Assistance with technical project design, light‑distribution simulations, and operational planning for solar and LED deployments.
Showroom experience — Design, implementation, and support for a dedicated TellCo showroom that showcases solar and LED solutions and strengthens local market presence.
Comprehensive training and capacity building — Access to training sessions, workshops, and seminars covering solar and LED technologies, franchise operations, and sales development.
Digital presence & marketing activation — Coordinated marketing campaigns and an exclusive TellCo Europe website with integrated e‑commerce to drive visibility and lead generation.
Financial and sustainability advisory — Expert guidance on financial planning, decarbonization strategies, and environmental compliance to help you build a strong and profitable franchise.
Brand reputation — Operate under an established international brand recognized for renewable energy, solar lighting, off‑grid systems, and consultancy services.
