Service Partnership Structure

Explore your service partnership opportunities

Installer Partners

For companies specializing in hands‑on deployment. Installers implement TellCo Europe LED, solar, cooling, and water systems with precision, following TellCo Europe’s certified standards. Perfect for technical teams ready to scale through consistent project flow.

EPC Companies

Ideal for engineering and construction firms delivering full turnkey projects. EPC partners design, procure, and build complete lighting or energy systems for municipalities, industrial clients, and large commercial sites.

TASPs — Technical Authorized Service Providers

The technical backbone of TellCo Europe. TASPs are certified partners authorized to install, commission, and maintain systems under warranty. They ensure long‑term performance, compliance, and brand protection.

Maintenance Partners

For companies focused on operations and maintenance. These partners handle preventive maintenance, repairs, monitoring, and SLA compliance — keeping TellCo Europe’s systems running flawlessly for years.

ESCO Partners

Performance‑driven partners who finance or co‑finance projects, guarantee savings, and deliver shared‑savings models. ESCOs are central to TellCo Europe’s clean‑energy transformation strategy and often manage large institutional clients.

EaaS / LaaS Operators

The highest‑value partnership category. Operators deliver TellCo Europe’s Energy‑as‑a‑Service and Lighting‑as‑a‑Service programs — managing financing, billing, monitoring, and full lifecycle operation of clean‑energy assets. This model creates recurring revenue and long‑term client relationships.

TellCo’s Premier Tier

ESCO & LaaS Operators — The Most Profitable Service Partnership

TellCo Europe has developed one of the strongest Energy‑as‑a‑Service (EaaS) and Lighting‑as‑a‑Service (LaaS) frameworks in the market. These programs eliminate CAPEX for clients, simplify procurement, and guarantee measurable savings — creating a powerful business model for operators.

Why ESCO & LaaS Operators choose TellCo

Offer zero‑CAPEX lighting and energy upgrades, enabling projects that traditional budget constraints would otherwise block.
Deliver guaranteed savings with smart metering and remote monitoring, making performance‑based contracts transparent, verifiable, and easy to validate.
Deploy Swiss‑engineered LED and solar technologies backed by extended warranties, giving clients confidence in long‑term reliability and measurable performance.
Serve high‑value sectors — municipalities, industry, hospitality, airports, ports, and government — where project sizes are larger and credit quality is stronger.
Provide off‑balance‑sheet financing through TellCo Europe’s global banking partners, enabling clients to modernize infrastructure without increasing debt or capital expenditure.
Execute projects using TellCo Europe’s proven end‑to‑end delivery framework — from feasibility and design to installation and long‑term O&M.
Build recurring revenue streams through multi‑year service contracts, transforming one‑time retrofits into predictable, stable cash flows.
Expand into larger, bundled project scopes by combining lighting, solar PV, battery storage, cooling, and water solutions into a single integrated performance contract.
