Efficient Lighting

LED Lighting Solutions

CLEAN POWER

Solar Home & PV Systems

Solutions: TellCoSOL ZERMATT Solar Home System; Solar PV; Off-Grid Add-On Products.

Scalable solar power systems that help customers improve energy independence, reduce electricity costs, and increase operational efficiency in connected, off-grid, and remote environments.

OFF-GRID ENERGY

ConSOL Off-Grid Containers

Solutions: Solar Container ConSOL; ConSOL Clinic Applications; Battery Storage.

Containerized off-grid energy systems designed for fast deployment, reliable power delivery, and efficient operation in remote, institutional, humanitarian, and mission-critical environments.

WATER & ENERGY EFFICIENCY

Solar Water Pumping Systems

Solutions: Solar Powered Water Pumps and Irrigation Solutions; Solar PV.

Solar-powered water pumping and irrigation solutions designed to maximize PV power use, reduce diesel dependency, lower operating costs, and deliver reliable water access in remote, agricultural, and harsh operating environments.

Solar Cooling Systems

Solar cooling solutions designed to support efficient refrigeration, temperature control, and energy savings in healthcare, agriculture, hospitality, and commercial settings.
